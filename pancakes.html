Fluffy Pancakes for breakfast

Main Menu

Description

Fluffy and airy pancakes perfect for a brunch, combine them with your favorite bacon, a couple of eggs and you have the perfect breakfast for two.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Aunt Jemima pancake mix
  • 1 & 1/2 cup of water
  • Cooking oil
  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Honey or mapple syrup

Instructions

  1. Mix the pancake mix with the water until you get a smoth mix
  2. Preheat the pan or griddle
  3. Oil the cooking surface and put 3/4 cups for each pancake
  4. Fry the bacon
  5. Use the bacon oil to cook the eggs to your liking
  6. Serve with honey or mapple syrup and Enjoy!
