Pineapple creamy jelly

Main Menu

Description

Sweet and creamy pineapple jelly

Ingredients

  • 1 can of sweet condensed milk
  • 1 can of pineapple chunks in it's own juice
  • 6oz of pineapple jelly
  • 1 Lemon

Instructions

  1. Boil enough watter according to the jelly instructions
  2. Mix the hot water with the condensed milk
  3. Use a blener to make a pineapple juice with the pineapple chunks
  4. Mix everything together and put in a pan
  5. Refrigerate for 4 hours and Enjoy
