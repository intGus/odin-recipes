Burnt-ends Recipe in the airfryer

Main Menu

Description

We're talking about Pork Belly Burnt Ends! The Best part of a good brisket is the fatty point and in competitions we turn those into mouth drooling Burnt Ends.

Ingredients

  • 8lb Pork Belly skin removed
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup of westchestersauce
  • Lemon pepper
  • Brown sugar
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions

  1. Remove pork belly from packaging and cube into 1" by 1" pieces
  2. Mix the pork belly with all the seasonings in a bowl
  3. Preheat the airfryer at 400f for 3 minutes
  4. Place the pork belly cubes with enough space between each piece
  5. Cook for 15 minutes, open and toss gently to expose more pieces to the direct heat
  6. After the 15 minutes, use another bowl to mix with some BBQ sauce and Enjoy!
